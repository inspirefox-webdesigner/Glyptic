An automatic fire detection system detects smoke, heat, flames, or gases and alerts people to a fire. It can also activate emergency protocols, such as calling the fire department and activating fire suppression systems. There are Fire Detection and Alarm System initially comes in the market as Conventional type, then Hybrid system and presently large infrastructures protected with Automatic Addressable type. Globally accepted product approvals & certification released with UL/ UL & FM/ VDS/EN54/LPCB/CE . Standards follows NFPA72 for application, Installation, location, performance, inspection, testing, and maintenance of fire alarm systems. Indian Standard code is IS 2189 , which provides a code of practice for the selection, installation, and maintenance of automatic fire detection and alarm systems.NBC- National Building Code recommend about Building.
