Fire Detection & Alarm System

We offer comprehensive fire detection systems including smoke detectors, heat detectors, flame detectors, and multi-sensor detectors. Our systems are designed to provide early warning and ensure maximum safety for your premises with advanced addressable and conventional fire alarm solutions.

Fire alarm systems should be inspected and tested monthly, with comprehensive maintenance performed annually by certified technicians. Regular maintenance ensures optimal performance and compliance with safety regulations. We provide AMC support for all our installed systems.

Yes, all our fire detection and alarm systems are fully compliant with local fire safety codes and regulations. We ensure proper installation and certification to meet all legal requirements and help you obtain fire NOC certificates.

Yes, we offer comprehensive training programs including Basic Fire Alarm Training and Advanced Fire Alarm Commissioning Training. Our classroom training sessions are designed to educate your staff on proper system operation and maintenance procedures.

Public Address System

An IP-PA (Internet Protocol Public Address) system uses network infrastructure to deliver audio announcements. It offers superior sound quality, easier installation, and better integration with other systems compared to traditional analog PA systems. Our integrated IP-PA & Talk Back Systems provide two-way communication capabilities.

Yes, modern PA systems can be seamlessly integrated with fire alarm systems to provide automatic emergency announcements. When a fire alarm is triggered, the PA system can automatically broadcast evacuation messages, ensuring clear communication during emergencies.

Digital PA systems offer superior audio quality, reduced interference, easier maintenance, and advanced features like zone-specific announcements, scheduled messaging, and remote control capabilities. They also provide better integration with modern building management systems.

CCTV System

We offer a comprehensive range of CCTV cameras including IP cameras, analog cameras, PTZ cameras, dome cameras, bullet cameras, and specialized flame and smoke detection cameras. Our industrial integrated IP-CCTV systems provide high-resolution monitoring with advanced analytics capabilities.

Storage duration depends on several factors including hard drive capacity, video resolution, compression settings, and number of cameras. Typically, systems can store footage for 7-90 days. We design storage solutions based on your specific requirements and compliance needs.

Yes, we offer specialized flame and smoke detection cameras that can automatically detect fire and smoke through video analytics. These integrated fire catcher cameras provide early warning capabilities and can trigger alarms or notifications when fire or smoke is detected in the monitored area.

Yes, our CCTV systems support remote monitoring capabilities. You can access live feeds and recorded footage from anywhere using computers, tablets, or smartphones. We also offer centralized command and control system setups for comprehensive monitoring solutions.
